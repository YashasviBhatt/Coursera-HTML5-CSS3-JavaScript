Tribute to Irrfan Khan

Introduction

Sahabzade Irfan Ali Khan (7 January 1967 â€“ 29 April 2020) or Irrfan was an Indian actor, known predominantly for his work in Hindi cinema, in addition to British and American films. Cited in the media as one of the finest actors in Indian cinema, Khan's career spanned over 30 years and earned him numerous accolades, including a National Film Award, an Asian Film Award, and four Filmfare Awards. In 2011, he was awarded the Padma Shri, India's fourth highest civilian honour.
